🌟 About Us – Learn With Nasya

An English learning platform born from love, built for everyone.

Learn With Nasya began as a personal project—a site I developed (partially using AI) to help my own daughter, Nasya, overcome her challenges in learning English. What started as a tool for one child has now grown into a joyful, accessible space for many.

This platform is designed to assist young children—especially non-native speakers—in reading, pronouncing, and understanding English words and sentences, in a way that feels fun, natural, and non-intimidating.

  • 📖 Read and explore everyday English
  • 🗣️ Practice pronunciation with ease
  • 💬 Receive real-time feedback with friendly guidance

By transforming language learning into delightful games, Learn With Nasya makes it easier for children to gain confidence and joy in their learning journey—at their own pace, in their own way.

🎯 Our Mission

Our goal is simple: to make language learning more accessible, especially for children. We aim to provide:

  • ✅ A platform anyone can use—anytime, anywhere
  • ✅ An intuitive experience, even for the youngest learners
  • ✅ A safe, minimal, and child-friendly interface

Every child deserves a kind, patient, and supportive environment to grow their communication skills—and that's what we're building.

🧠 Powered by Smart Technology

Leveraging speech recognition, text-to-speech, and interactive feedback, Learn With Nasya acts like a gentle tutor—providing encouragement and corrections without pressure or judgment.

👶 Who Is It For?

  • 👧 Children learning English for the first time
  • 👪 Parents who want to support their kids’ learning at home
  • 🎓 Teachers looking for engaging digital tools
  • 🌏 Anyone seeking a simple way to learn or teach basic English

💡 Why We Do This

Because learning a language should never feel like a burden—it should feel like play. Learn With Nasya is our small gift to all the children out there discovering the joy of words. We invite you to join us in this journey of playful learning.
